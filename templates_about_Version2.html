About Genome Project

What is the Genome Project?

A research initiative to analyze genetic traits in populations and understand dominant characteristics.

History

The Genome Project started as an effort to map and analyze human genetic diversity, with applications in medicine, anthropology, and forensics.

Usefulness / Applications

  • Medical research
  • Personalized healthcare
  • Anthropological studies
  • Trait prediction
